“Hazrat Shaykh Zulfiqar Ahmad Naqshbandi Mujaddidi (db)”

Upon Waking Up from Sleep

Read:

اَلۡحَمۡدُ لِلّٰهِ ٱلَّذِي اَحۡيَانَا بَعۡدَ مَا اَمَاتَنَا وَاِلَيۡهِ ٱلنُّشُوۡرُ

All praise be to Allah سبحانه وتعالیٰ who bestowed life upon us after death and towards Him is our return.

Translation

All praise be to Allah سبحانه وتعالیٰ who bestowed life upon us after death and towards Him is our return.
